WORDLEby Aswan

1

Enter the first word

To get started, simply enter any five-letter word to find out which letters match the hidden word. In total, you will have 5 tries to guess the hidden word.

2

Find out what letters are in the hidden word

If any letters are marked in the accent color, this means that this letter is in the hidden word, but doesn't match the exact location in this word. If any letter is marked in the darkest color, then it is in that word and is in the exact location. If the letters are marked in the light color, then it isn't in the hidden word.

3

Try to guess the hidden word

Now if you know a few letters with the exact location (darkest color) and a few letters that are in the word (accent color) you can try to solve the hidden word and win the game!
